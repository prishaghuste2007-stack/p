Our Story

Two Decades of
Crafted Spaces

Founded in 2003 by Rohit & Dipali Ghuste, both alumni of Rachana Sansad Mumbai, Design Basics has grown over 23 years into one of Mumbai's most distinguished luxury interior studios. We specialize in creating sophisticated, bespoke environments that seamlessly blend aesthetics with functionality — reflecting the unique aspirations of those who live and work within them.

Our commitment to excellence, deep understanding of spatial composition, and meticulous attention to every element have earned us a reputation for timeless, tailor-made interiors that stand the test of time.

Rohit Ghuste

An exceptional command of spatial flow, proportions, and design harmony — crafting modern spaces that balance aesthetics with comfort.

Dipali Ghuste

An extraordinary eye for texture, color, and intricate detailing — bringing depth and distinctive character to every space.

Press & Recognition

Featured in Creative Indian Offices Coffee Table Book

Design Basics was prominently featured for our work on Ocean Chemicals — a 205 sq. meter Mumbai office designed on the philosophy of 'less is more.' Glass partitions replacing primary walls lend fluidity and visual connectivity, while a controlled use of mustard on a monochromatic palette delivers a suave, refined result.

The project stands as a study in strong lines, intuitive color theory, and intelligent spatial planning — hallmarks of our commercial design approach.
